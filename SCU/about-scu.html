About Game & Wiki

Set in the year 2531, the game primarily tells the story of the state of humanity - and the galaxy - 1 year after the destruction of the Tau Ceti Colony. From commanding a starship across star systems, to exploring exoplanets and visiting alien civilisations, the adventure awaits you in SCU: Online.

"Star Core Universe: Online" is an Adventure, MMORPG game developed by a team of writers and developers, to bring to life a science fiction universe written by leokayasen.

Using this wiki, you can find out lore about the ships, factions, items and so much more! You can start either by clicking on one of our Trending Topics or by exploring the options in the navigation bar above.
